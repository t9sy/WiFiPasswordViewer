# WiFiPasswordViewer

**WiFiPasswordViewer** is an incredibly handy utility that lists every single Wi-Fi network your computer has ever connected to, alongside their clear-text passwords.

Perfect for when a friend comes over and you can't remember your own Wi-Fi password, or when you need to connect your phone to a network your laptop is already on.

## Features
- Extracts saved Wi-Fi SSIDs and their security keys using native `netsh` commands.
- Displays everything in a neat, easy-to-read table.

## Usage
1. Right-click `ViewWiFiPasswords.ps1`.
2. Select **Run with PowerShell**.
3. View your passwords! (Admin rights may be required to view the clear-text keys depending on your UAC settings).
